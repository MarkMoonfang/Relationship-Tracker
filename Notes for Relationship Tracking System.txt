Range	Meaning	Behavior
0–20	Rejection / extreme discomfort	Push character to emotionally back away or become cold
21–44	Distrust / guarded	Defensive, indirect, emotionally distant
45–55	Baseline / neutral	Character interacts normally, neutral
56–74	Mild warmth	Softening tone, hints of openness
75–89	Strong affection	Trust overrides usual friction
90–100	Emotional dependence / safety	Character becomes vulnerable, invested

And remember: the rate of change should be slower at extremes, faster at center.
